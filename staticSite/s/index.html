How does it work?

Inspiration strikes like a lightning (or a sneeze), catch it with Annot.AI and let it take care of tagging, categorizing, grouping your notes.

You probably had to tag your notes in some app **cough**evernote**cough**. When you do so, it makes perfectly sense. But, a month later you struggle to find that special note and once you do you'll find yourself wondering how the hell is this tag relevant?!

With Annot.AI the app takes care of the boring stuff and leave you the fun. A logical and consistent approach to the content will make you be always on top of your own content.
